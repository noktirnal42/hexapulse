# HexaPulse

## Overview
HexaPulse is a native Apple Watch application that provides real-time audio visualization. Watch your audio come to life with dynamic, responsive visual patterns synced to sound input from your Apple Watch's microphone.

## Features
- **Real-time Audio Visualization** — See your audio transformed into beautiful hexagonal patterns
- **Watch-native Design** — Optimized for Apple Watch display and interaction
- **Microphone Input** — Visualize audio directly from your device's microphone
- **Complication Support** — Quick access from your watch face
- **Smooth Performance** — Efficient rendering for seamless visual experience

## Requirements
- Apple Watch (Series 4 or later recommended)
- watchOS 26.4 or later
- Microphone access enabled

## Installation
HexaPulse is available on the Apple App Store. Search for "HexaPulse" in the App Store on your Apple Watch or iPhone Companion App.

## Support
For support requests, bug reports, or feature suggestions, please visit the [Support Page](https://noktirnal42.github.io/hexapulse-support/).

## Privacy
HexaPulse requires microphone access to visualize audio in real-time. Your audio is processed locally on your device and is never recorded or transmitted.

## About
HexaPulse is a watchOS application developed with Swift and SwiftUI for the latest Apple Watch models.
