Privacy Policy

HexaPulse — Last Updated: April 2026

Introduction

HexaPulse ("App," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our watchOS application.

Key Point: HexaPulse does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or audio recordings. All audio processing happens locally on your device.

Information Collection and Use

Audio Data

HexaPulse requires microphone access to visualize audio in real-time. However:

  • Audio is processed only on your device
  • No audio data is recorded or stored
  • No audio is transmitted to our servers or external services
  • No audio recordings are created

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information such as:

  • Name or contact information
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Health information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

Automatic Information

HexaPulse does not automatically collect device data, analytics, or crash reports. The app operates entirely offline with no network connectivity required.

Permissions

HexaPulse requests only the following permission:

  • Microphone Access: Required to capture audio for real-time visualization

You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your Apple Watch settings without affecting other app functionality (though audio visualization will not work).

Data Security

Since HexaPulse does not collect or store any personal data, there is minimal risk of data breaches. Your audio remains entirely under your control on your device.

Third-Party Services

HexaPulse does not use any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates independently without external integrations.

Data Retention

HexaPulse does not retain any data. Audio is processed in real-time and is not saved to your device or transmitted anywhere.

Children's Privacy

HexaPulse does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, children can use the app safely with parental supervision.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Control microphone access through your Apple Watch settings
  • Delete the app and all associated data at any time
  • Contact us with privacy questions or concerns

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of HexaPulse constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Compliance

HexaPulse is designed to comply with:

  • Apple's App Store Privacy Policy requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations

Since HexaPulse collects no personal data, you maintain complete control over your information at all times.
