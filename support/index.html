HexaPulse

Real-time Audio Visualization for Apple Watch

Welcome to HexaPulse Support

HexaPulse is a native Apple Watch application that transforms your audio into beautiful, dynamic hexagonal visualizations in real-time. This support page provides information about the app and how to get help.

Key Features

  • Real-time audio visualization with hexagonal patterns
  • Native watchOS design optimized for Apple Watch
  • Microphone input for live audio capture
  • Complication support for quick access from your watch face
  • Smooth, efficient rendering for great performance

System Requirements

  • Apple Watch Series 4 or later (recommended)
  • watchOS 26.4 or later
  • Microphone access enabled on your device

Privacy & Security

HexaPulse respects your privacy. The app requires microphone access to visualize audio in real-time, but:

  • Audio is processed locally on your device only
  • No audio data is recorded or stored
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • You control microphone permissions through watchOS settings

Getting Started

Step 1: Download HexaPulse from the Apple App Store on your Apple Watch or iPhone Companion App.

Step 2: Grant microphone permissions when prompted.

Step 3: Open the app and enjoy real-time audio visualization!

Step 4: Optional: Add the complication to your watch face for quick access.
